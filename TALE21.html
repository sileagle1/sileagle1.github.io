TALE 21 - Celestial Celebration

Come to our 21st TX Annual Letterbox Event (TALE) as we celebrate all things celestial. This includes objects, tools and events, or anything else pertaining to astronomy. Since a total solar eclipse will be visible across a large swath of Texas on April 8, it seems like a good reason to celebrate our place in the cosmos. So come help us make this even out of this world!                                                                               

Date:  Saturday, 03/02/24

Time:  10:00 am - 09:00 pm

Location:  Group Dining Hall in McKinney Falls SP - Austin, TX

Directions:  From I35 go east on E William Cannon Dr for 3.4 miles then left on McKinney Falls Pkwy for 1.2 mile to park entrance on the left at 5808 McKinney Falls Parkway. Pay fee and get a trail map, then continue straight past camping areas to the Group Dining Hall near the Amphitheater.

Details:  Sign up for this event on Atlas Quest so we have a headcount. We will begin by exchanging, stamping the event stamp, and obtaining clues. We will have a copier to print clues for boxes placed during the event. There will be a potluck lunch, so bring food to share if you want to participate. We will also play TX Bingo around 06:30 pm Saturday, a game where you stamp a card with TX symbols carved by Baby Bear and hope I call them in the right order for you to win a prize. We will provide prizes, but voluntary donations and money for expenses are appreciated. FYI no pets allowed in the Hall.

Accommodations:  There are tent/RV sites and a few cabins that can be reserved online. Hotels are in Austin within Travis County.

Other Nearby Activities:  State Capital Tour and Lady Bird Johnson Wildflower Center.
