Series: TX Birding Trail

The Great Texas Coastal Birding Trail winds through the entire Texas coastal region and offers access to some of the state's best bird and wildlife viewing. This letterbox series will take you to some of my favorite areas of the trail and provide a diverse set of stamps, too. Even if you are not a birder, I think you will enjoy visiting these places, so grab your camera and binoculars and get started!
