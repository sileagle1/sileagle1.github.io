Series: TX Baseball

Texas has played a crucial role in the evolution of our national pastime, from town teams in the 1860's and the formation of the Texas League in 1888, to current major league teams the Houston Astros and Texas Rangers. After reading the book "Texas Baseball" by Clay Coppedge, I decided to create this series to pay tribute to the teams and players of Texas. Enjoy!
