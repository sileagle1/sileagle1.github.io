Series: Literature

Reading has always been one of my favorite things to do, and since I also enjoy letterboxing, I decided to combine the two and create this Letterbox Series. It includes some of the interesting characters, magical places, and famous titles found in the wonderful world of literature. So, find a box, then read the book!
